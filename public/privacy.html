Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This WhatsApp booking assistant ("the Service") helps salons schedule appointments with their customers over WhatsApp. This policy explains what information we handle and how.

Information we collect

When you message a participating salon through WhatsApp, we process: your WhatsApp phone number, your name (if you provide it), the messages you send to book or manage an appointment, and the appointment details (service, date, and time).

How we use it

We use this information only to schedule your appointment, answer your questions about the salon's services, and send you appointment confirmations and reminders. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

Data sharing

Your information is shared only with the salon you are booking with, and with the service providers required to operate the assistant (WhatsApp/Meta for message delivery, and our hosting and AI providers for processing your request). We do not share it with anyone else.

Data retention

We keep appointment and conversation records only as long as needed to provide the service to the salon. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting the salon or us.

Your choices

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying to ask us to stop. You may also request a copy or deletion of your data.
