# First outreach batch — ready to send

**Your live demo link:** https://studio-bella-demo.onrender.com
(Tip: open it once before a DM session so the free host is awake — first visit can take 30–60s.)

**Daily target:** 10–15 personalized DMs/day · aim for 2–3 replies → 1 call/week.
Log every prospect in the Prospect Tracker as you go.

---

## Channel rules
- Send via **Instagram DM** (or email). Never cold-blast WhatsApp.
- Personalize the first line with something real about their salon (a recent post, their service, their city).
- One message, short. Lead with the outcome, not the tech.

---

> Hola [Nombre] 👋 Soy Rafael, desarrollador aquí en [tu ciudad].
>
> Vi que en [Salón] atienden por WhatsApp/DM — armé un asistente que contesta a tus
> clientas al instante y agenda las citas solo, 24/7, para que no se pierda
> ninguna por no alcanzar a responder.
>
> Puedes probarlo tú misma aquí (30 seg): https://studio-bella-demo.onrender.com
>
> Si te gusta, te lo dejo funcionando para [Salón] esta semana. ¿Te interesa? 🙌

## Follow-up #1 — 2–3 days later (no reply)

> Hola [Nombre], ¿pudiste ver el demo? 👆 Contesta y agenda solo, hasta de noche y
> domingos. Si quieres te lo dejo listo para [Salón] sin compromiso.

## Follow-up #2 — the "break-up", ~4–5 days later

> ¡Sin problema si ahora no es el momento, [Nombre]! Te dejo el link por si lo
> quieres probar luego 👉 https://studio-bella-demo.onrender.com  ¡Muchas citas! 😊

(Then stop — no fourth message.)

## When they reply with interest
Move to a short call (use the discovery talk track + objection handling in the
Outreach Kit). Offer the Founding Pilot: discounted setup + ~$79/mo in exchange
for a testimonial.

## Optional: personalized Loom
For higher-value prospects, record a 60-sec Loom: say their salon name, then show
the demo booking a cita. Send the clip instead of (or with) the link.
